Jordan 1's

The Jordan 1 was Michael Jordans first custom shoe. Jordan 1's first released from 1985-1986, but 1994 after Michael Jordan's retirement the Jordan 1 kickstarted the retro era. It wasnt until 2008 that the Jordan brand started re-releasing the high cut Jordan 1's. Right now the Jordan 1 is one of the hottest shoes on the market with its many different colorways.
